NAMAY ACCESSORIES

In this design created by the Namay brand, natural and biodegradable materials were incorporated to demonstrate a commitment to preserving the Earth. Harmful materials for the environment were avoided. The design focused on using the macramé knotting technique to give the bag a 100% handmade look. The bag frame is made of wood, and various colors are used to resemble the blue macaw. The specific macramé knot used is the 4-knot, which not only adds strength to the weave but also creates an aesthetically pleasing design.
