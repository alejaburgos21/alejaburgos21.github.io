NAMAY ACCESSORIES

In this design created by the Namay brand, natural and biodegradable materials were incorporated to demonstrate a commitment to preserving the Earth. Harmful materials for the environment were avoided. The design focused on using the macramé knotting technique to give the bag a 100% handmade look. The bag frame is made of wood, and various colors are used to resemble the blue macaw. The specific macramé knot used is the 4-knot, which not only adds strength to the weave but also creates an aesthetically pleasing design.

ORCHARD AT HOME

This project was carried out at the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic with the intention of creating a natural environment at home during lockdown. A balcony garden was designed in an apartment, and this design was crafted using recycled materials, specifically milk cartons. These cartons are made of cardboard on the outer side and have an inner layer of aluminum, which makes them difficult to recycle. However, in the garden, they serve as a waterproof barrier for the planters’ irrigation system, as the material won’t deteriorate quickly due to water exposure.
